How it works

  • Each character in the target vocabulary word becomes its own slice unit.
  • Auto mode detects a mostly one-direction stroke as slicing and a looping path as lassoing.
  • Lasso closes itself with a straight line if the path does not fully reconnect.
  • Hyphens stay in the sequence and can be sliced on either side.
  • Slicing through the body of a character tile is rejected.
  • Long chunks and notes resize to stay inside the slicing container.
  • This template only keeps the slicing window and related logic.
